Main Dish Ideas

1. Grilled Halal Beef Steak

Ribeye or sirloin (Zabiha halal). Season with garlic-infused olive oil, rosemary, black pepper, salt, paprika. Serve with roasted carrots & lactose-free mashed potatoes.

2. Crispy Fried Chicken (Egg-Free)

Halal chicken thighs/drumsticks. Coat in rice flour or gluten-free flour mix (paprika, cumin, salt, pepper). Fry in suitable oil. Serve with safe fries.

3. Chicken Skewers

Grilled chicken marinated in ginger, turmeric, garlic-infused oil, salt, pepper. Serve with safe peanut sauce (check ingredients) and rice.

4. Low FODMAP Chicken Bowl

Grilled chicken strips, jasmine rice, roasted zucchini/carrots. Optional: Max 1/8 avocado.

5. Stuffed Bell Peppers (Egg-Free)

Fill peppers with seasoned ground halal beef, rice (optional), carrots, spinach, chives. Top with lactose-free cheese. Bake until tender.

6. Low FODMAP Butter Chicken

Use garlic-infused oil, ginger, appropriate spices. Creamy sauce with lactose-free yogurt or coconut cream. Serve with rice or GF naan.

7. Halal Kofta Patties

Ground beef/lamb mixed with cumin, coriander, paprika, chives, salt, pepper. Pan-sear. Serve with Low FODMAP sides (e.g., cucumber salad, rice).

8. Low FODMAP Chicken Wrap

Use rice paper or gluten-free tortilla. Fill with grilled chicken, lettuce, chives, safe dressing, maybe a few avocado cubes.

Okay-List Fruits (Low FODMAP Portions)

Stick to recommended serving sizes:

  • Pineapple (fresh, canned in juice - check portion)
  • Mango (very small portion - check Monash app)
  • Blackberries (very small portion - check Monash app)
  • Strawberries (fresh - check portion)
  • Oranges / Mandarins
  • Firm Bananas (ripe bananas are high FODMAP)

Always check the Monash FODMAP app for current portion sizes.

Foods to Avoid / Limit

Common High FODMAP / IBS Triggers:

  • Avocado (limit to 1/8 portion max)
  • Garlic & Onions (use garlic-infused oil instead)
  • Eggs (based on your preference)
  • High FODMAP Fruits (apples, ripe bananas, pears, watermelon, etc.)
  • Lentils, Beans, Chickpeas (unless prepared to reduce FODMAPs, small portions)
  • Cauliflower, Broccoli (check portion sizes)
  • Wheat, Rye (unless sourdough or specific GF products)
  • Dairy (unless lactose-free)
