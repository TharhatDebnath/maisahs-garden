🍖 Main Dishes — Big Girl Meals

1. Grilled Halal Beef Steak

Ribeye or sirloin, Zabiha halal. Seasoned with garlic-infused olive oil, rosemary, black pepper, paprika. Cooked medium or well-done. Served with roasted carrots & mashed potatoes (lactose-free).

🔥 Why it rocks: Classic, juicy, super savoury

2. Crispy Fried Chicken (Egg-Free)

Halal chicken thighs or drumsticks. Coated in rice flour or gluten-free flour with spices (paprika, cumin, turmeric). Fried in canola or avocado oil. Side idea: Garlic-infused fries 🍟

🔥 It’s crispy, spicy, and just satisfying.

3. Chicken Skewers (Satay Vibes)

Grilled chicken with ginger, turmeric, garlic oil. Dip: FODMAP-safe peanut sauce (no garlic or honey). Serve with basmati rice or Low FODMAP quinoa salad.

🔥 Satisfying & simple. Eat with hands for full joy.

4. Avocado Chicken Bowl (Low FODMAP Style)

Grilled chicken strips, Jasmine rice, Roasted zucchini, carrots. 🥑 Avocado: Max 1/8 sliced on top (perfect portion for IBS and its cute and tiny!)

🔥 Creamy, warm, and totally your style.

5. Stuffed Bell Peppers (Savory & Egg-Free)

Filled with seasoned ground halal beef, carrots, spinach, chives. Topped with lactose-free cheese. No rice needed — just the good stuff.

🔥 Melty and flavorful inside every bite.

6. Butter Chicken (Low FODMAP & Halal)

No onion or garlic — uses garlic oil and ginger for depth. Creamy sauce made with lactose-free yogurt or coconut cream. Served with rice or gluten-free naan.

🔥 A cozy meal for cold days, totally tummy-safe.

7. Halal Kofta Patties

Made with ground beef or lamb. Seasoned with cumin, coriander, paprika, chives. Pan-seared in olive oil. Served with Low FODMAP tabbouleh (no onions) + yogurt dip (lactose-free).

🔥 Spicy, juicy, & just hits right.

8. Low FODMAP Chicken Wrap

Rice paper or gluten-free wrap. Grilled chicken, lettuce, chives, safe dressing, maybe a few avo cubes.

🔥 Grab-n-go vibes, savory and snacky.
